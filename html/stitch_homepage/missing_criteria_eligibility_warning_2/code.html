Role Overview

The Senior Finance Officer plays a critical role in the Financial Strategy division, overseeing the preparation of complex budgetary frameworks and advising senior executives on fiscal management within the Department of Treasury and Finance.

Key responsibilities include financial reporting compliance, coordination with external auditors, and leading a small team of analysts focused on departmental expenditure forecasting.
